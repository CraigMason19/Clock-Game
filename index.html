How to play
  • The goal is to identify the right time-zone / location from three analog clocks.
  • There will be one correct clock and two random wrong clocks.
  • If you play in Strict mode, you will only have one chance to guess correctly. In Loose mode, you have two chances to guess correctly. Choose whichever mode is the most fun for you!
  • Once the correct answer has been given, a Google Image Search link will open with the keyword location to show the location.
Time-zones

Random timezone clocks are generated using data from the Internet Assigned Numbers Authority (IANA).

There are around 450 time-zones to chose from in the IANA.
